Definition
Average Potential evapotranspiration minus precipitation (PE-P) for period from May 1 to August 31 (same as the existing LSRS) (mm). (Must be multiplied by -1 for use in LSRS to get P-PE).

Method of calculation
Sum of average daily PE-P for defined period. PE is calculated using the Baier-Roberson formulae by default, or as selected by the user.
