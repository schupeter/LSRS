Definition
Average seasonal Aridity index (cm) for soil with 200 mm water-holding capacity, 10% probability level

Method of calculation
The index is computed from the water deficit (PE-P) in a manner similar to that used by Sly and Coligado (1974). Note that Aridity is in cm, water deficits are in mm. A regression equation is used to compute Arid200-10 as follows:
Arid200_10 = -7.11 + 0.13182*(Deficit5) – 0.00007854*(Deficit5)**2
Note: Regression estimates should not be used for Deficit5 values that exceed 900mm.
