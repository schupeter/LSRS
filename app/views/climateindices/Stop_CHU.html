Definition
Average date of end of CHU accumulations (calendar day)

Method of calculation
Stopping date is based on the date when the mean daily minimum air temperature (Tmin) drops below a threshold value as defined by the user. Threshold temperatures estimate the 10% probability date of first fall freeze (-2ºC) and presently vary from 3.7 to 6.5 ºC for different regions across Canada (see Appendix 1).
